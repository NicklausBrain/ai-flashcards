# Product Guidelines: AI Flashcards (My1kWordsEe)

## Design Principles
- **Clarity First:** The UI should be clean and uncluttered, prioritizing the learning content (Estonian words and sentences) above all else.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast for text, clear buttons for interactive elements, and responsive design for use on mobile devices.
- **Feedback-Rich:** Provide immediate visual and auditory feedback for correct and incorrect answers in games.
- **Consistency:** Use a consistent color palette and typography across all game modes and pages to build user familiarity.

## Visual Style
- **Color Palette:** Clean and modern. Primary colors for actions, with distinct colors for feedback (e.g., success/failure in games).
- **Typography:** Sans-serif fonts (like those provided by Blazor Bootstrap) for maximum readability.
- **Imagery:** AI-generated images should be relevant to the context of the Estonian word or sentence and maintain a consistent aesthetic where possible.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Gamification:** Reward user progress with visual indicators (e.g., progress bars, badges) and provide a sense of accomplishment through game levels.
- **Error Tolerance:** Allow users to easily retry failed exercises and provide helpful hints when they are stuck.
- **Seamless Navigation:** Simple, intuitive navigation between the word list, flashcards, games, and the favorites page.

## Content Guidelines
- **Estonian-First:** Always emphasize the Estonian word or sentence, with English translations provided for support.
- **Accuracy:** All translations and AI-generated sentences must be verified for grammatical correctness and natural phrasing.
- **Tone:** Encouraging, educational, and supportive.
