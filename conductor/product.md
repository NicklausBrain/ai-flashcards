# Product Definition: AI Flashcards (My1kWordsEe)

## Product Vision
A modern, interactive platform for learning and memorizing the top 1,000 most common Estonian words. By leveraging cutting-edge AI for content generation (text, images, and speech), the app provides a rich, multi-sensory learning experience that goes beyond traditional flashcards.

## Target Audience
- Language learners of Estonian (specifically beginners and intermediate learners).
- Students preparing for language proficiency exams (A1, A2, B1).
- Expats living in Estonia seeking to improve their daily vocabulary.

## Core Goals
- Provide a curated list of the most essential Estonian vocabulary.
- Gamify the learning process through various interactive exercises (translation, listening, matching).
- Ensure high-quality, contextual examples using AI-driven sentence generation.
- Offer native-sounding audio feedback and visual aids to reinforce learning.

## Key Features
- **Flashcard System:** Interactive cards with Estonian words, translations, and AI-generated sample sentences.
- **AI Media Generation:** Automatic generation of contextually relevant images (Stability AI) and speech (Tartu NLP) for every word and sentence.
- **Interactive Games:**
    - **Translate to English/Estonian:** Test translation skills in both directions.
    - **Listening Practice:** Transcribe spoken Estonian generated by AI.
    - **Word-to-Word Matching:** Match Estonian words with their English equivalents.
    - **Noun Form Games:** Practice the three main forms of Estonian nouns.
    - **Word Grind:** Practice a custom set of words by filling in blanks in AI-generated sentences.
- **User Progress Tracking:** Ability to mark words as "known" or "favorite" to focus on areas needing improvement.
- **Custom Word Sets:** Create and save personalized lists of words to "grind" through interactive exercises.
- **Topic-Based Learning:** Organize words into logical categories (e.g., food, travel, daily life).
